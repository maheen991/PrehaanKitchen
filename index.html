About Prehaan

With over 15 years of experience in some of the world's most prestigious kitchens, Prehaan brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to every dish he creates. His journey began in the bustling streets of Mumbai, where he first discovered his love for the culinary arts.

After training under Michelin-starred chefs across Europe and Asia, Prehaan developed a unique style that blends traditional techniques with innovative flavors. His philosophy centers on using the freshest ingredients to create dishes that tell a story.

Prehaan's Kitchen was born from a desire to share this expertise with home cooks and aspiring chefs alike. Whether you're looking to master the basics or elevate your skills to professional levels, Prehaan's approachable teaching style makes gourmet cooking accessible to all.
